FAQ
  • What is a fork?
    Fork is an opportunity to bet on all results of the competition in different bookmakers and make a profit regardless of the result of the competition.
  • How to use the site?
    You just need open a several bookmakers site, choose common event on each site and write odds on our site. For your convenience, you can change the names of sites and teams, so as not to make a mistake when writing coefficients.
